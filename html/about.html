About NowOnCampus

We're dedicated to connecting campus communities through seamless event discovery and management, making every campus experience more engaging and accessible.

Our Mission

NowOnCampus was born from the simple belief that campus life should be vibrant, accessible, and connected. We've seen too many students miss out on amazing opportunities simply because they didn't know about them.

Our platform bridges the gap between event organizers and participants, creating a unified space where the entire campus community can discover, engage with, and manage events that matter to them.
